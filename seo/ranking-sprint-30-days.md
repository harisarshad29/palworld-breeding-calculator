# 30-Day Ranking Sprint (Execution)

## How Google Tracks Backlinks

Google discovers backlinks when:

- Googlebot crawls external pages that link to your site
- your links appear on pages already in Google's index
- Google Search Console updates the Links report after recrawl

Important: backlink reporting is delayed. A link today may appear in GSC after days/weeks.

## Daily Tasks (Days 1-30)

1. Publish/refresh 1 high-intent page daily:
   - `/pal/:name` pages
   - `/combo/:a/:b` pages
   - guide pages
2. Send 5 outreach messages daily using templates in `seo/outreach-templates.md`.
3. Log all outreach and live links in `seo/backlink-tracker.csv`.
4. Submit newly published URLs in Google Search Console (URL Inspection).

## Weekly Tasks

1. Review Search Console:
   - impressions
   - CTR
   - average position
   - indexed pages
2. Rewrite meta title/description for pages with high impressions + low CTR.
3. Build internal links from top pages to weaker pages.
4. Add 1 comparison/guide page targeting long-tail terms.

## Backlink Quality Rules

- Prefer niche-relevant domains over random high DR sites.
- Avoid spammy PBN/comment links.
- Target contextual links inside article body.
- Keep anchor text natural (brand + partial match mix).

## KPI Targets (30 days)

- 100+ indexed URLs
- 30-60 quality referring domains
- 3x growth in impressions
- 1.5x-2x growth in clicks
